Problem Statement

What Problem Does This Invention Solve?

Existing cloud storage systems mostly depend on static lifecycle policies, manual rules, or centralized optimization. They do not adapt properly to changing workloads, often ignore carbon intensity, and may require raw cloud data to be transferred to a central server. This creates cost, energy, privacy, and sustainability challenges.

Privacy Risk

Centralized AI requires raw cloud data transfer.

Carbon Ignored

Carbon data is monitored but not used for automatic decisions.

Inefficient Resources

Storage, compute, network, backup, and replication are not optimized together.
